Agarwal Packers and Movers – Raipur offer and specialized a flexible, competitively priced removals and packaging service for residential and commercial in within India and overseas to any destination around the globe, regardless of whether you have one item or a complete household or commercial to send by road, sea or air.

What to do on Shifting Day?
You must sort out the goods which you would not like to take with you. All old papers, worn out machineries, broken furniture can be sold out or left behind. Make a list of all goods that you would be taking with you to the new destination. This will also help you in verifying whether your all goods have reached to the destination and that no any part is missing.
